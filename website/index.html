Help & Documentation

How to Get LinkedIn Cookie

  1. Log in to LinkedIn in Chrome/Firefox
  2. Right-click anywhere and select "Inspect" (or press F12)
  3. Go to the "Application" tab in Chrome or "Storage" in Firefox
  4. Under "Cookies", find "www.linkedin.com"
  5. Look for the "li_at" cookie and copy its value
Never share your LinkedIn cookie with untrusted applications or websites.
